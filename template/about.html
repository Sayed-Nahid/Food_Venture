Who we are

Food Ventyre is a leading locall online food delivery marketplace, connecting consumers and restaurants through its platforms. Offering consumers a wide variety of food choice.

As an online food delivery marketplace, Food Ventyre facilitates the online ordering, payment and occationally, fulfilmentent of orders. We utilise a hybrid model which builds on our marketplace heritage (where restaurants do their own delivery) with logistics capabilityty (for select restaurants without their own delivery capabilitesties). This modell ensures consumers have the widest possible variety of restaurants to order from.
